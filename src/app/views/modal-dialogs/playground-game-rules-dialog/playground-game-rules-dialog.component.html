Welcome to the Playground!

Playground Layout

  1. This is the entire playground's layout.
  2. The topmost section displays the 'timer' representing the remaining time left to perform the action each round.
  3. The top section just below the 'timer' is the opponent section.
    While the bottom section represents the player section.
  4. The left section represents the 'void-zone' which can be used to take a look at the discarded cards.
  5. The right section represents the 'deck-zone' which represents the count of remaining cards in the deck.
    It also contains an option to quit the game.
  6. The mid bottom section shows the Player Cards which will flip on hover. There is also toggle switch on the Pick or Choose section to flip all cards at once.
  7. The bottom right section includes an information on the Bounty amount as well as the opponent's Bounty amount.

Playground Layout - Player Section

  1. The top section belongs to the Opponent player while the bottom section belongs to the Player.
  2. The bottom left portion indicates the Pick or Choose section.
    During the Pick game phase the Player would either Pick options i.e. 3 Lies and 1 Truth. (always both)
    During the Choose game phase the Player would either Choose either 3 Lies or 1 Truth. (never both)
    It contains a toggle switch to flip/re-flip the Cards. It also contains a button to toggle between Truth or Lies option list.
  3. The mid bottom section shows the Player Cards which will flip on hover. There is also toggle switch on the Pick or Choose section to flip all cards at once.
  4. The bottom right section includes an information on the Bounty amount as well as the opponent's Bounty amount.

Playground Layout - Void Zone

  1. This Void-Zone is present on the left section of the playground.
  2. The Void Zone contains the collection of discarded cards.
  3. When you hover over the same and if there are any discarded cards then you can view these cards in the mid section of the playground.
    When you click the button shown on hover over the 'Void' deck, then a list of all the discarded cards are displayed in the mid section.
    This allows the player to study the cards discarded so far to increase the percentage of picking the correct choice!
  4. Do note that only '4 lifelines' are provided allowing to analyze the discarded cards!

Playground Layout - Card Deck

  1. This Deck-Zone is present on the right section of the playground.
  2. The Deck Zone contains the collection of playable cards.
  3. One can forfeit the game anytime by click on the button that appears when hovering over the 'Deck'.
  4. Once forfeited the player forfeiting would lose the game and will be redirected to the home page!
